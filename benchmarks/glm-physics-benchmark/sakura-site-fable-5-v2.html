Story

物語

Born from a single brushstroke, our story follows the old masters of sumi-e — where an entire season lives in one gesture of ink. Each spring, we return to the same hillside, the same gnarled bough, to witness what cannot be kept: the five-day bloom, the slow white drift, the quiet after.

Collection

作品集

Hand-ground ink on washi paper. Limited seasonal works — blossom studies, wind sketches, and the falling-petal series — each sealed with the vermilion mark of the atelier. No two strokes repeat; no two springs return.

Visit

来訪

The atelier opens with the first bloom and closes with the last petal. Find us where the river bends beneath the old cherry trees — arrive slowly, leave quietly, and let the season do the speaking.
