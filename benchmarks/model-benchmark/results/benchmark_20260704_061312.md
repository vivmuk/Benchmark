### Key Findings:
1. **Context Window**: MiMo-V2.5 has 1M tokens (4x larger than Kimi's 262K)

- **Primary (code tasks)**: Kimi K2.7-Code — code-specialized, 30% fewer reasoning tokens

- **Long-context tasks**: MiMo-V2.5 wins with 1M context window
- **Cost-sensitive tasks**: MiMo-V2.5 is much cheaper
