 ## 1. Root Cause Chain

Most likely chain of failure:

1. **Redis hit its `maxmemory` limit** and was configured with `noeviction` (or no expiry on keys).  
   → Redis started rejecting writes with `OOM command not allowed`.
2. **Login path lost its cache/session/rate-limit layer.** Requests that normally hit Redis fell back to the database, or the app retried Redis writes while holding DB connections.
3. **Database concurrency spiked.** With `max_connections=20`, the pool filled (`active_connections=20, waiting_connections=5`).
4. **New login requests blocked/timed out** waiting for a DB connection.
5. **API returned 500** on the login endpoint.

In short: **Redis OOM was the trigger; DB connection-pool exhaustion was the downstream symptom that produced the 500s.**
