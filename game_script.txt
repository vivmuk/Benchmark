Title: Elephant Adventure

Welcome to Elephant Adventure, a simple game where you will navigate through a magical forest, encounter friendly elephants, and complete challenges to earn points!

Game Overview:
- You will start at the entrance of the forest.
- You will encounter various obstacles and challenges.
- You will meet friendly elephants who will give you hints and help.
- Your goal is to reach the end of the forest and collect as many points as possible.

How to Play:
1. Read the description of each challenge.
2. Choose the correct action to overcome the challenge.
3. Collect points for each correct action.
4. Avoid mistakes to lose points.
5. Reach the end of the forest and see your final score.

Let's start the game!
